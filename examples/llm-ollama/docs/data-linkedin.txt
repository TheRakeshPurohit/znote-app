
LinkedIn is a professional social networking platform founded in 2002 and launched in 2003. It is mainly used for professional networking, job searching, recruiting, personal marketing and sharing content related to the world of work. LinkedIn allows users to create detailed professional profiles, connect with other professionals, join topic groups, follow companies, post articles and updates, and search for job openings.

The platform has millions of users worldwide, making it an essential tool for professionals across all industries and experience levels. It is widely used by job seekers to find career opportunities, by recruiters to identify qualified candidates, by businesses to promote their brand and find business partners, and by professionals to expand their network and share their knowledge. knowledge.

In 2016, Microsoft acquired LinkedIn for approximately $26.2 billion, and since then, the platform has continued to grow and evolve to meet the changing needs of the professional market.
